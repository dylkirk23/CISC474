My Projects

Plate Builder

This was my senior design project that spanned two semesters. I worked with Frontier Scientific as well as six other
students to make a plate builder that allowed users to easily add and edit specific compounds they needed mixed.
I helped on nearly every aspect of this project, but my main focus was making sure the main functionality with the
grid itself worked well and was intuitive to use.



Dino Jumper

This project was for my Advanced Web Technologies class where we learned about web deelopment and what makes a
webpage look good and easy to navigate. I worked with a small group and we made a simple platformer game where
the player controlled a small dinosaur and had to complete puzzles and collect all the coins while avoiding
enemies in order to progress to the next level. My part in this was making sure we had consistency across
all of our different pages as well as level design and playtesting.
